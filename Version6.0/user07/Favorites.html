Subnautica

Summary:

Subnautica is an underwater open-world survival game. You play as a crew member on the Aroura, which ends up crash-landing on an alien planet. Collect resources to survive and venture deeper do discover the secrets of this planet.

Minecraft

Summary:

Minecraft is an open-world 3d survival game with practically no limits. Every world is randomly generated and everything is cubic. There are no rules or limits other than your imagination.
My review: One of my top favorite games of all time. It brings me so many good memories. 9.9/10
